Is a trip to Detroit on your list?

Known as the "Motor City," Detroit has long been the heart of America's auto industry, home to giants like Ford, GM, and Chrysler. The city's innovation didn't stop with cars; it's also celebrated in sports, boasting legendary teams across all major leagues. From unique Coney Island hot dogs to the bustling Eastern Market, Detroit offers a culinary scene that’s rich with history and flavor.

Sports, Food, and Culture

Sports:

Detroit is a powerhouse in sports, with 22 championship titles across its major teams. The city is home to iconic teams like the Pistons, Lions, Red Wings, and Tigers, each contributing to Detroit’s celebrated history in American sports.

Food:

Eastern Market, a vibrant community hub, reflects Detroit’s rich cultural diversity through food. From locally grown produce to international flavors, the market showcases the city’s multicultural roots. Don’t miss out on iconic Detroit staples like Coney dogs, square pizza, and locally brewed craft beers.

Culture:

Detroit’s museums celebrate the city’s innovative spirit. The Detroit Institute of Arts (DIA) houses world-class art collections, while the Henry Ford Museum captures the essence of American progress. Whether you're an art lover or history enthusiast, Detroit’s museums offer a deep dive into the city’s cultural fabric.
